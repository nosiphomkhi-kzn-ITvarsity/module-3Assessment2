Granadilla Lemonade

Prep Time: 5 mins

yield: 2

Ingredients






Steps

1.In a pitcher add the passion fruit, lemon juice, water and simple syrup (simple syrup is dissolving.

and 1 part sugar in 1 part boiling water and letting it cool down). Stir to combine and add more simple syrup if necessary.

2. Refrigerate to cool and serve over ice.
