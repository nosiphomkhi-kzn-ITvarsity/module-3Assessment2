Chicken Sekay Noodle Bowl

Prep Time: 15 mins

yield: 4 serving

Ingredients









Steps

Step 1 Prepare noodles following packet directions. Drain.

Step 2 Meanwhile, combine lemongrass, ginger and 2 teaspoons peanut oil in a glass or ceramic bowl. Add chicken. Rub to coat.

Step 3 Heat a large frying pan over medium heat. Cook chicken for 3 to 4 minutes each side or until cooked through. Transfer to a plate. Cover to keep warm.

Step 4 Heat remaining peanut oil in pan over medium-high heat. Add eschalot, garlic and chilli. Cook for 4 minutes or until onion has softened. Add satay sauce and coconut milk. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to low. Simmer for 5 minutes or until slightly thickened. Remove from heat. Stir in sesame oil.

Step 5 Slice chicken. Divide noodles and cucumber among 4 bowls. Top with chicken. Spoon over satay sauce. Sprinkle with peanuts, extra chilli and coriander. Serve.
