A Juicy Beef Stake

Prep Time:15 minutes

Yield:6

Ingredients










Steps

1. First, make the marinade. Whisk all the marinade/dressing ingredients into a bowl. Marinate the steak. Place the steak into a shallow dish, and pour 1/3 of the prepared marinade/dressing.

2. Gently massage the steak to evenly distribute the marinade on all sides. Then, allow it to sit for 15 minutes, to get to room temp.

3. Cook the steak. On a grill or in a pan, on medium heat, cook the steak on both sides, for about 5 minutes in total for a medium-rare. Or cook until your level of doneness. Then, allow resting for 10 mins Assemble the salad. Next, combine the veggies in a salad bowl, then add sliced steaks and pour the remaining dressing on top. Finally, garnish with crumbled cheese, toss well and serve
